Hello, I am a second year sociology major currently attending the University of California, Riverside. Some of my freetime is spent baking, painting, or lounging around with my family and friends. I have many passions in life but a major one is to help those in need of help. Some points in life can be very stressful and that may be hard on a lot of people, however, i am hoping that my aid to them can make them see their world in a brighter light. I am interested in tutoring troubled kids or teens that struggle with getting their studies down or even understanding their studies at all.

With my majoring in sociology I am given the ability to study the behavior of an indivudal. I get to learn about the many reasons people do certain things and the over all aspect of it. Due to my interest in studying the human behavior it has made me more fascinated to tutor these kids and at the same time understand their behavior. It makes me feel a lot closer to them and it gives me a sense of purpose, as if i have the power and knowledge to guide these children in the correct path.

My goal is to make sure all those in need of help know that there are people out there willing to help them with whatever they need. Most times a kid will be embarassed to say they need assistance and that is normal but, as a community we need to teach our kids that asking for help is not embarrasing it is responsible.
